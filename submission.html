About This Workshop

We invite researchers to submit papers and posters to our workshop on Uncertainty in Open-World Robotics. This workshop brings together researchers tackling uncertainty across various domains in open-world robotics, with a focus on addressing uncertainty from a practical perspective to develop methods for real-world robotic systems operating in dynamic and unstructured environments.

We welcome submissions covering a broad range of topics related to uncertainty in robotics. Your contributions will be presented as posters, with selected papers chosen for highlight talks.

Topics of Interest

Open-World Perception:

  • Out-of-Distribution / Open-Vocabulary/ Anomaly Detection and Segmentation
  • Uncertainty Estimation for Machine Vision
  • Transfer Learning and Domain Adaptation
  • Continual and (Inter-)Active Learning on Robots
  • Self-supervised Learning in Deployments

Uncertainty in Robotics:

  • Uncertainty Quantification in Robotic Perception and Control
  • Safe Decision-Making under Uncertainty
  • Uncertainty-Guided Exploration and Planning
  • Confidence Estimation and Calibration
  • Sensor Fusion and Uncertainty Propagation
